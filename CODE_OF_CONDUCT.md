# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

AgentVerse is an open-source project for researchers, builders, and developers working on haptic intelligence and robotics. We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and productive community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but accept correction when impact differs from intent.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its technical goals.
- Give clear, actionable feedback.
- Respect maintainers' time and project direction.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Trolling, repeated disruption, or bad-faith arguments.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Abuse of project infrastructure, issues, discussions, or communication channels.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or block participants who violate this code of conduct. Serious violations may be escalated to the relevant platform administrators.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers. Include relevant links, screenshots, or context where possible.
